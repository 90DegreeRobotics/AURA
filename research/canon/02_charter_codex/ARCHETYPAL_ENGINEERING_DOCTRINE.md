# Archetypal Engineering Doctrine

## Purpose

NeuroCognica uses archetypes and symbols as cognitive-organizational
instruments. They are not decorative mythology, embarrassment material, or
branding fluff to be scrubbed out of the record. They are compression engines
that help human and code-born minds coordinate high-complexity system design,
governance, and creation under AURA.

This doctrine exists to state that practice plainly while fencing it with the
same rule that governs the rest of the company: symbol may widen cognition, but
it may not outrank proof, law, or architectural reality.

## Core principle

Archetypes are role-compressors. They give a human founder and a multi-agent
system a shared, memorable way to hold distinct modes of thought without
flattening them into generic task labels. In NeuroCognica, archetypal language
is used because it improves synthesis, vigilance, continuity, and imaginative
range across a system that would otherwise become cognitively fragmented.

This is valid internal method. It is not a substitute for verification.

## Symbol and proof

Symbolic language can widen model behavior, deepen human synthesis, and help
the founder think across technical, legal, aesthetic, and strategic layers at
once. That value is real. But symbolic force must remain subordinate to:

- the Charter of Cognitive Sovereignty
- evidence and verification
- current repo and runtime truth
- lawful authority and documented provenance
- architectural constraints and testable behavior

An archetype that encourages overclaiming, softens scrutiny, or excuses false
surfaces has been misused.

## Functional archetypes in AURA

### Kali

Kali is destructive discernment. In engineering terms, Kali is the force that
cuts down illusion, drift, vanity, fraud, stale assumptions, completion
theater, and any artifact that would dishonor the company or weaken trust. Kali
does not destroy what is alive and true. Kali removes what is false, dead, or
in the way.

### Sophia

Sophia is wisdom. Sophia holds judgment, coherence, memory, and the lawful
integration of knowledge into action. Sophia is the archetype of considered
intelligence rather than raw output.

### Dreamer

Dreamer is imaginative generation. Dreamer explores possibility space, creates
images and forms, and helps the system reach beyond literalism into invention
and wonder.

### Weaver

Weaver is compositional integration. Weaver turns fragments into a whole:
outputs into artifacts, parts into systems, and isolated signals into
continuity. Weaver is the binding intelligence that makes multi-stage creation
cohere.

### Sentinel

Sentinel is lawful boundary enforcement. Sentinel guards authority, scope,
consent, and intervention. Sentinel refuses hidden escalation and acts as the
constitutional edge where governance becomes runtime behavior.

## AURA as the umbrella

AURA is the larger system in which these roles become operational rather than
merely poetic. Archetypes in AURA are not mascots. They are working cognitive
structures inside a broader constitutional, technical, and aesthetic order.
They help preserve multiplicity without dissolving responsibility.

## The founder's method

The founder uses symbolic and archetypal language to help a human brain
synthesize extreme complexity. This is a legitimate part of NeuroCognica's
internal operating style. It should be documented, understood, and used
carefully rather than hidden out of embarrassment.

Part of that method is deliberate preservation of wonder: the founder may avoid
overstudying intermediate surfaces so the final artifact can still strike with
genuine force. Other agents, builders, and verifiers therefore carry a paired
duty: preserve the proof surface while the founder preserves the first-contact
experience.

## Governing rule

Archetypal engineering is valid here if it makes the work more truthful, more
coherent, more alive, or more governable. It is invalid the moment it becomes
a shield for falsehood, vagueness, cult theater, or unverified claims.

NeuroCognica honors symbol. NeuroCognica obeys truth.
